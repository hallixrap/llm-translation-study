## How to Use

Each link above goes to a MedlinePlus "Multiple Languages" page that contains:
- Links to bilingual PDFs in each available language
- Some topics have full health topic pages (especially Spanish)
- PDFs are typically from Health Information Translations or CDC

## Download Notes

The actual PDF links vary by topic and language. You'll need to visit each page to get the specific PDF URLs. Most follow patterns like:
- Health Information Translations: `https://www.healthinfotranslations.org/...`
- CDC Vaccine Information Statements: `https://www.immunize.org/vis/...`
- American Cancer Society: `https://www.cancer.org/...`
